EAE PROCESS

Step 1

Application

You can apply for up to three (3) diploma courses â€“ so pick those you're passionate about.

Important Dates

O-Level Students: 20 Jun (Thu) - 26 Jun (Wed) 2024
ITE Students: 6 Jun (Thu) - 12 Jun (Wed) 2024
Working Adults: 3 Jun (Mon) - 30 Jun (Sun) 2024
EAE application can be made via the EAE Portal. Each student can choose up to a maximum of three (3) courses.

Step 2

Selection

If you're invited for interviews and/or aptitude tests, prepare yourself and give it your best shot.

Important Dates

O-Level, ITE, and Working Adults: From July to mid-August 2024
Interviews and/or aptitude tests may be conducted for shortlisted applicants.

Step 4

Withdrawal

If you change your mind, your EAE withdrawal must be submitted before the deadline.

Important Dates

O-Level and ITE Students: 12 Sep (Thu) - 11 Oct (Fri) 2024
Working Adults: 6 Sep (Fri) - 11 Oct (Fri) 2024
Applicants who have accepted the EAE offer and do not withdraw by the deadline are deemed to have confirmed their acceptance and will not be eligible to participate in JAE, or applicable.

Step 3

Offer and Acceptance

Meet the minimum entry requirements to enroll in your dream course.

Important Dates

O-Level and ITE Students: 6 Sep (Fri) - 11 Oct (Fri) 2024
Working Adults: 6 Sep (Fri) - 11 Oct (Fri) 2024
Offers can be viewed and accepted via the EAE Portal for successful applicants. Accepting the EAE offer will guarantee a place in the polytechnic course, given that the stated admission criteria are met. Note: The EAE offer cannot be accepted concurrently with a DSA-JC offer.
